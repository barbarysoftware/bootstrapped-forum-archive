I am organizing a regular meetup in Swindon (UK) for people who are running (or are interested in running) their own bootstrapped software product business. Come along and talk shop with other aspiring and experienced bootstrappers. It doesn’t matter if you are developing for web, Windows, Mac or mobile.

The first meetup is at a pub on the evening of Tuesday 16th June 2015. You can find out more and RSVP at http://www.meetup.com/South-West-Bootstrappers/ . If any of you are within reach, I would be great to see you there!

Andy February 5, 2016, 10:53am #8

About 20 people turned up for the first one. I thought it went great.

Lots of people signed up for the 2nd one, but only 4 turned up (including me).

Only 5 people turned up for the 3rd one.

About 80 people had registered with the group, but only a fraction of them ever showed (cunningly meetup.com encourages members to join lots of and then charges you more if you have more members).

So I stopped paying the sub. No-one else took it over and meetup.com has deleted the group. It’s a pity it didn’t work out, but you don’t know until you try.
