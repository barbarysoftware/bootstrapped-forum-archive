As a general remark: There are some good training options for bootstrappers. The thing is however that there is a requirement of the training to fit with your personality, style of learning, business type and stage you are in.

As an example, I consider Seth Godin’s Marketing Seminar top notch for bootstrappers, but I can imagine that for others this Marketing Seminar would be a waste of time and money.
So, how do you choose? You’ll know only afterwards.

Some resources:

  • https://30x500.com/academy/
  • https://themarketingseminar.com/
  • http://www.earwolf.com/show/startup-school/ (really good and free)
  • https://nugget.one/

and probably many others.
