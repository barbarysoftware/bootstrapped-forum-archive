Like to ski? Like MicroConf discussions? We have the perfect match for you…Big Snow Tiny Conf East and West, the just-right-mix of skiing and mastermind-style hallway track discussions you have at your favorite conferences! It’s getting to be that time of year again–Registration will open shortly, so if you want to be on the “early access” list, you should sign up here (for West, held in Beaver Creek, Colorado, Feb 5-8, 2018):
https://bigsnowtinyconfwest.com/signup/

And here (for East, held in Sugarbush, Vermont–January 22-25, 2018):
https://east.bigsnowtinyconf.com/ (scroll to the bottom)

(Yes, the info on the site is currently out of date for East–@bradt is on it and will update soon)

Announcements will go out to these lists on October 2, so make sure you’re on one if you’d like to attend!

Hope to see you there.

Great concept and a great conference. We held the first annual European version last year and it was a ton of fun. This year we’re doing the 2nd annual BSTC Europe in Avoriaz, France on February 5-8.

Great chance to get together with fellow bootstrapped founders, talk some shop, do some skiing, and enjoy a few days together with a few of your new best business friends.

Even if you can’t make one of the events I highly suggest this type of thing for everyone. SO different than the bigger conferences, and a great compliment to other events you might have planned.
