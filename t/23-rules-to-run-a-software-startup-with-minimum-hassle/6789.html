I use Paddle.

The first thing to realise is that you can‚Äôt compare Paddle with Stripe, they‚Äôre in different categories.

Stripe and Braintree are Payment Gateways. They let you get money from credit cards and leave the rest to you, including retries, charge backs, VAT and invoices. The customer buys directly from you.

Paddle and FastSpring are Merchants of Record. They resell your product and handle everything including taxes and invoices and you get one wire transfer once a month with your earnings, minus their cut. You don‚Äôt have any customers, they do.

Frankly, I don‚Äôt see why anyone running a SaaS business would consider Stripe, it‚Äôs just so much more work. My friend says his developer team spent two months setting everything up.
