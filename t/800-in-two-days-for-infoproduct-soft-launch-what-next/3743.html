akash January 4, 2016, 5:52am #2

Congratulations on launching and making your first five sales. This is an awesome niche to be in and I think there is a lof of demand for guides/tutorials for devops.

Next set of moves

  1. Reach out to the 5 customers who purchased your guides. Speak to each one of them, preferably over the phone. You want to build a “customer persona” based on these (and the next 20-30 customers), so that you can perfect your marketing message over time. Specifics to get from these customers - what do they do, where do they work, what benefit did they expect from the guide, did it meet their expectations and other important yada yada

  2. Look at what others are doing in this space. e.g. Serversforhackers.com seems to be quite known in the dev community. While researching this, think about how you want to position yourself. Also see how they market themselves to their customers. (note: devs hate being directly marketed to)

  3. The first 5 customers reached your landing page from your personal twitter network. Trust was already built before they saw the landing page. Before you start advertising to people who don’t know you, you should look at adding some trust signals to your landing page - e.g. images of the people giving testimonials (you can simply use a twitter card and show the relevant tweet here), put up an image of you and your team at Keming Labs, put up specific information on you that markets you as an expert in dev ops - the more relevant information I get about you, the more I trust you. (when targeting the dev community, being simply open and human is a great marketing technique)

  4. Get feedback on the landing page from your target market - messaging, more trust signals, make buttons stand out etc

  5. Start running small amounts of google, facebook, twitter ads. Track conversions, so that you can figure out which marketing channel works best for you.

Once you have tested out multiple advertising channels and found which one works the best, double down on it and go make more sales!
