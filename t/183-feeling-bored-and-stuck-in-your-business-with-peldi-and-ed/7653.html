My honest feedback: I thought your co-host looked great, and made a lot of really thoughtful points.

In seriousness, I vote you go for it. But decide what the goal of your podcast is. Is it to share your personal story and opinions? Is it to promote your business? Is it a tool to educate your customers or attract potential customers? Obviously, more than one answer is possible and it can evolve over time, but I do think you should know the goal(s) rather than just hit record and start marching with an uncertain destination. Set an achievable goal, say 20 episodes, and some sort of success metric - though that could just be ‚ÄúI am having fun‚Äù, and then see if you get there. If yes, keep going., if not stop or adjust the goal.
