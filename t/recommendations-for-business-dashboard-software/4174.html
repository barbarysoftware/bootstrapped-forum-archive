FWIW, there seem to be a two broad categories I’m seeing:

General info radiators

These usually take the form of ultra-customizable widget-based dashboards that can take input from a lot of different sources.

  • Geckoboard
  • Cyfe
  • Klipfolio
  • Dasheroo

FWIW I did a trial of Klipfolio and I’ve been impressed with the level of depth in it. Particularly the fact that it’s possible to connect it to pretty much anything that has a RESTful API without writing any code.

Subscription analytics

These plug into Stripe, Recurly, or one of a few other subscription payment processors. They are focused strictly on deep financial analytics, starting from MRR and getting more detailed from there.

  • BareMetrics
  • FirstOfficer
  • ProfitWell

I’m thinking I’m probably going to wind up picking one from column A and one from column B - the dashboards seem nice for keeping track of more than just financials, but none of them dig as deeply into financials as the finance-focused products.
