DazeEnd September 21, 2015, 5:25pm #2

Thanks Andrey. I’ve been a long-time listener to Bootstrapped (and a lurker on these forums), so I really appreciate the introduction.

As Andrey mentioned, I’m helping organize Release Notes, which is a conference about the business of iOS and Mac development. There’s a lot of overlap between that topic and other fields like the business of Android, SaaS, and .Net development, though, so we hope and expect to have some “cross-pollination” with indies and bootstrappers from other disciplines represented. We also recognize that a lot of the business of the software business is done as consulting, so we’ll have talks and discussions about not just product work, but also independent contractors.

We’d love to have a contingent of Bootstrapped.fm listeners at Release Notes, so please let me know if you have any questions. Tickets are available on our website (http://releasenotes.tv/conference), but sales will be ending on Oct 12th. We hope to see you there!
