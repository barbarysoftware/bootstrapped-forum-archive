Reporters/journalists don’t (or at least, shouldn’t) expect or accept payment from the people they write about.

If they accept payment, then it is an advertisement (or possibly an ‘advertorial’).

MrAndyChris August 2, 2016, 4:34am #4

I’ve had some success without a great deal of effort on https://www.helpareporter.com/

The Process Breakdown Podcast episode with Conrad Egusa has some useful tips.

I was speaking to a very successful friend of mine about this a week or two ago and his experience is that it’s actually not that hard. You just need to find journalists who are already talking about the problem you solve and then pitch them a unique angle on it. He also pointed out, that like cold calling / emailing for sales, it is something of a numbers game.
