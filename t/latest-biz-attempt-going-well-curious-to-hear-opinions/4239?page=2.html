Design wise, if you don’t want a theme, follow some of the guidelines from http://webtypography.net/

The thing that will make the biggest difference:

body {
    max-width: 33rem;
    margin-left: auto;
    margin-right: auto;
}

Anything like the above, to center and keep your line-lengths legible will make a massive difference.
