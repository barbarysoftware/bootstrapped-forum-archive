An often overlooked but IMO very important criteria for product ideas and pricing models: the pricing should scale/increase with the value the customer gets.

E.g. base the price on the number of users customers have for a team-based product such as help desk software, sales management app, dev tool and so on. This way it‚Äôs much easier to grow customer accounts to higher subscription fees as they get more and more value out of your product. It‚Äôs also much easier to sell a product for $50/month and grow this to $500/month over months/years as the customer gets more value out of the product, instead of selling a product for a higher price from the start.
