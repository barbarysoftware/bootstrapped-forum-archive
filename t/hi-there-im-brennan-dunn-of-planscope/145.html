My name is Brennan Dunn, and I’m the founder of Planscope, a project management app for freelancers and teams of consultants.

I also work on…

  • Freelancer’s Weekly, my newsletter with 6.5k+ subscribers (essentially my primary customer acquisition channel for everything I do.)
  • Author of “Double Your Freelancing Rate” and “Sell Yourself Online: The Blueprint”
  • I teach a monthly workshop on consulting (and do occasional one-off workshops)
  • I host The Business of Freelancing podcast

The newsletter, books, and podcast have worked amazingly well to bring in new Planscope customers (> 60% of new subscribers come from one of the above), and I’ve also had a lot of luck ranking for freelance-specific keywords.

I’m working on some more team-focused reporting features right now, as $199/mo >>>>> $24/mo (plus, freelancers tend to have a very hard time staying in business, and the first thing they do when work dries up is cancel things like Planscope.)

Cheers,
