We make customer training accessible.

Marble is a customer training platform for small and medium-sized companies to educate their customers with minimum costs while connected with customer success strategy.

If your business spends a lot of money on face-to-face training of the customers, you have no time on adopting complex learning management systems or spend weeks on content creation, then Marble is for you.

So, why Marble?

Companies become successful learning providers and build learning communities around their products.

Marble is a new generation learning platform which helps companies to create embedded academies, educate their customers with an intuitive training builder.

It is designed and created by learning experts and PhD in education technology. We combine most effective teaching approaches based on research and advanced user interface for customer centered education.
