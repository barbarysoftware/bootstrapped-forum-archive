CasJam April 3, 2014, 4:57pm #2

For my SaaS, Restaurant Engine, they are:

  1. Organic search traffic
  2. Direct (I guess mostly “not provided” search traffic & word-of-mouth direct input)
  3. Clicks from our email newsletters
  4. A handful of industry-related referring sites
  5. Some paid channels

In the very very early days, I ran some low-budget Adwords to build a pre-launch list. That was my very first traffic source.

Within 3-6 months, organic search traffic took over, thanks to a content marketing strategy.
