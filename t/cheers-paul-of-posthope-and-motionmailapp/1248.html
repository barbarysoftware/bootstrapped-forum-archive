Paul March 1, 2014, 9:05pm #4

Thanks @andrey, @gavin for the welcome.

A client at work was doing a big product launch and our email designer designed a countdown timer as a part of their teaser campaign. I ended up throwing the server side code together for generating the timers just for this campaign. After the campaign was over, I did some competitive research and really could not find anything on the market. In the end, lack of competition, demand and the fact that most of the people looking for this feature run e-commerce businesses was enough to make it a SaSS.
