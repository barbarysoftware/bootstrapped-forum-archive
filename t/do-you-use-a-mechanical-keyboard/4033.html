Below is the slightly edited version of an email I send to the developer a while ago. Of course something similar could be done using some setup on the host machine, but never quite as easily as it works with the Ergodox, so this time is the only time I really use it. Anyway, the email (additions I made to it for this post are marked with ‚ÄòNOTE‚Äô):

  • form factor - I have another support under the wrist rests because with the tent legs, the wrist rests aren‚Äôt tall enough to keep the top of my hand in line with my lower arm.
