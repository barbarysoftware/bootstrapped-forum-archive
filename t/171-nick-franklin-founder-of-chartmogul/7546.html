#171: Nick Franklin, founder of ChartMogul

Bootstrapped Podcast
SteveMcLeod March 19, 2021, 3:29pm #1

Nick Franklin and I talked about what it is like to go down the venture capital route instead of bootstrapping.

For me this was a glimpse into another way of doing things.

Nick and I discussed:

  • why he used outside investment to create ChartMogul
  • ChartMogul’s focus on creating good content
  • finding a steady stream of interesting things to write about
  • the difficulty of knowing how customers are acquired
  • getting pricing right
